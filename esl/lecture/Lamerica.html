Lecture - History: Vikings and America

Key Leaders and Voyages

Leif Erikson (c. 970 – c. 1020)
Son of Erik the Red (founder of Greenland colony).

Sailed from Greenland to North America. Called the new land “Vinland” — possibly modern-day Newfoundland(Canada) or further south. Known as the first European to set foot in North America (not counting Greenland).

The Vinland Sagas:
Two medieval Icelandic texts:
Saga of the Greenlanders
Saga of Erik the Red
Describe Norse explorations to a land west of Greenland. Mention encounters with native peoples (called “Skrælings”).
